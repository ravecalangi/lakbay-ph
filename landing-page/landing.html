About

One app for every Filipino traveler

Lakbay was built out of a simple frustration — having to juggle five different apps just to plan a day trip. Weather here, tourist spots there, a chatbot somewhere else.

Now it's all in one place. Search any city, get live conditions, browse tourist attractions, and ask an AI for local tips — in Tagalog or English.
